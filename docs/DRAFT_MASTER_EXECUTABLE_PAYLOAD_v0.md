## Payload

### 1. The Meta-Ontology

- Ontology (O), Epistemics (E), Deontics (D), and Utility (U) are irreducible
  structural dimensions of any world that becomes intelligible, actionable,
  governable, or evaluable.
- Transcendent O remains real; however, every articulated O is mediated,
  partial, and recursively conditioned by E, D, and U.
- Theories are not innocent maps; they are U-driven productions of situated
  agents. U is the axis through which O-E-D structures become directionally
  real in action.

### 2. The Compiler Pipeline

- O: what is the object, claim, or system boundary?
- E: what is the provenance, access route, and justification?
- D: what is permitted, required, or binding relative to the E-shaped picture
  of O?
- U: what optimization pressure, stake, or preference is guiding the artifact?

### 3. Adaptive Domain Routing

- Hard/physical domains: `O -> E -> D -> U`
- Soft/reflexive domains: `U -> D -> O -> E`
- Rule-based domains: `D -> O -> E -> U`

### 4. Agentic Delegation And Discourse

- Agents receive a task-bound, brokered session: a precisely typed fragment of
  user agency, not portable identity substance.
- Local inferences must be evaluated against the task truth condition currently
  in force, severed from global training genesis.
- Epistemic abuse occurs when one party manipulates another party's E not to
  improve truth-tracking, but to illegitimately suppress, capture, or deform
  the other party's U.

### 5. The Civilizational Horizon

- Lived reality is experienced bottom-up: `Utility -> Deontic -> Epistemic ->
  Ontological`.
- A personal ODEU assistant must serve as a deontic-epistemic sentinel,
  modeling the user's reflectively endorsed U-profile to defend against
  external U-capture.
- No external system may rewrite a person's utility more deeply than that
  person has licensed.

### 6. The Failsafe

- ADEU must audit ADEU.
- The system cannot persist by opacity.
- It must consolidate under self-disclosure or terminate under adequately
  disclosed failure.
